Welcome to Coderox AB

Coderox was founded in Vallentuna, Sweden in August 2011 by Johan Lindfors after several of years working at Microsoft and also a short period with Infozone. After having been one of the most familiar faces in the Swedish developer community, evangelizing the Microsoft technologies and products, Johan decided it was time to build something new and exciting.

CODEROX OFFERINGS

Coderox core assets are designing and developing mobile solutions and applications - primarily for the Microsoft client platform, and providing developer related consulting services:

  • App and solution development
  • Architectural guidance and recommendations
  • User Interface and User Experience design
  • Cross-platform development targeting iOS, Android and Windows with Xamarin
  • Game development
  • Web application development with ASP.NET and related technologies
  • Cloud-based applications with Windows Azure and Office 365
  • Training, workshops, labs and custom presentations

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

With long experience in the overall Microsoft platform and technologies, Coderox develops solutions reaching from the cloud into the mobile devices. Coderox also have knowledge in developing games.

SOLUTION ARCHITECTURE

Code rocks, but having a good design and architecture is vital to an applications or solutions success. Security, performance, UX, testing, patterns and data access, Coderox talk the talk and walk the walk.

TRAINING & COACHING
